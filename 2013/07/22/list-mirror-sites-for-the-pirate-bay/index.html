List: mirror sites (proxies) for The Pirate Bay

22 Jul 2013

Today two more domains were blocked by ISPs. This is a worrying trend in internet censorship. A free internet should be protected!

I'm responding to this by sharing a lot of links to other torrent sites and mirrors of The Pirate Bay (the most famous torrent site) and links to some other lists and some other torrent sites. I also had a mirror of The Pirate Bay, but it doesn't seem to be working at the moment.

If you don't know about torrenting, there's a bittorrent guide on Lifehacker.com

Torrent sites

Links to some torrent sites that I like. The most famous is The Piratebay, but I find Isohunt.to to be very good. The Pirate Bay is usually not available directly - see below.

  • isohunt.to (secure)
  • torrentbox.sx
  • scrapetorrent.com
  • sumotorrent.com
  • torrentus.to
  • come.in proxies
  • piratebay.so (usually blocked)
